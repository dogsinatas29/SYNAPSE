SYNAPSE v0.2.12 introduces the **Intelligent Context Vault**, designed to completely eliminate the friction of documenting your AI-assisted development logic.

### Simply Press `Ctrl+Alt+M`
- **Zero-Click Capture:** Say goodbye to manually copying and pasting your LLM chat history. SYNAPSE now quietly monitors your VS Code Copilot sessions in the background.
- **Seamless Workflow:** Start your recording with `Ctrl+Alt+M`. Interact with your LLM and write code normally. When you press `Ctrl+Alt+M` again to finish, your latest AI conversation and Git diffs are instantly merged and saved to your project `.synapse_contexts/context.md` file natively.
- **No Popups:** Completely native flow. No input boxes, no distracting modals. Focus entirely on your architecture.
